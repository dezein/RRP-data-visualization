Description: Amendment prohibits any funds authorized in the bill from being allocated to any private, nongovernmental, or multilateral organization that directly or indirectly performs abortions in a foreign country, except in instances of rape, incest, or if the life of the mother is endangered; and prohibits any expenditure of funds authorized in the bill for the UN Population Fund, unless the Preisdent certifies that the organization has terminated all activity in China.

42. H.Amdt.404 to H.Amdt.403   — 104th Congress (1995-1996)
Description: Amendment to the Smith (NJ) amendment (A005) sought to specify that the prohibitions contained in the amendment shall not be applicable to the medical treatment of injuries or illnesses caused by unsafe abortions; prohibit the use of funds for abortion, but allow organizations that perform abortions to use funds authorized in the bill for family planning and health services other than abortion; and limit continued funding for the U.N. Population Fund by requiring the President to certify that the organization does not support coercive abortion and that no U.S. funds provided to the UNPF have been used for activities in China.
